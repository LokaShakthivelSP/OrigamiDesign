Origami

Origami is the art of paper folding, which is often associated with Japanese culture. In modern usage, the word "origami" is used as an inclusive term for all folding practices, regardless of their culture of origin.

Camel

Interesting facts about Camel

There are two types of camels:
One humped Dromedary camels and
two humped Bactrian camels.

They have three sets of eyelids and
two rows of eyelashes to keep
sand out of their eyes.

Chameleon

Interesting facts about Chameleon

Skin crystals enable them to change
colour.

Unlike lizards, they can't regrow
their tails.

Cicada

Interesting facts about Cicada

There are around 3,000 cicada species.

Most cicadas only have four to
six weeks of activity before they die.

Panda

Interesting facts about Panda

An adult can eat 12–38 kilos
of bamboo per day!

Their eyes are different to
normal bears.

Pigeon

Interesting facts about Pigeon

They are incredibly complex and
intelligent animals.

They are highly sociable birds.

Interesting facts about Teddy
